Conversational AI for Everyone

AI Checking & Chapter Intelligence

Answer sheets are evaluated automatically — saving teachers hours weekly. The AI then maps every score to the exact chapter and concept, so you know precisely where each student needs to focus — not just which subject, but which page.

⚠️

Early Warning System

Identifies at-risk students before it's too late — tracking declining marks, attendance drops, homework gaps, and ranking falls. Every student gets a risk score, weak subject list, and engagement level — visible to teachers and admins instantly.

📊

Assessment, Homework & Performance

Every submission, score, and engagement signal is captured automatically. Live dashboards give teachers, students, and admins a distraction-free view of KPIs, ranking trends, homework rates, and evaluation history — all in one place.

🏫

Admin School Intelligence

Principals and admins get a full school-level view — class rankings, teacher effectiveness trends, bottom 2 performers per class, question quality scores, and department signals. Ask anything. Get answers from real data, not spreadsheets.

Student & Parent Visibility

Students see their rank, subject-wise progress, and chapter gaps in real time. Parents can ask where their child stands, which chapters need focus, and track homework consistency — without waiting for a parent-teacher meeting.
